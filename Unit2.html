The Technology Systems

Unit Details

How do technology systems work? How do they impact your daily life? This unit provides an introduction to technology systems and their role in the modern world. Building on your existing knowledge, you will explore the components and functions of technology systems, including hardware, software, and networks. You will investigate how technology systems support various applications and services, such as communication, data storage, collaboration, search engines, and blogging.
